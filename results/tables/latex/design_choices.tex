Time embedding
  & Time2Vec & Encodes days since first measurement using learned linear and sinusoidal functions. Captures long-term temporal trends and periodic patterns. \\
  & Delta-t & Encodes log-compressed time difference between consecutive measurements. Represents irregular sampling. \\
\midrule
Health state encoding
  & Binary & Encodes each value as within or outside the population reference range. Does not distinguish high vs.\ low. \\
  & Ternary & Encodes whether the value is low, normal, or high relative to the reference range. Handles asymmetric risk patterns. \\
\midrule
Age encoding
  & Raw values & Uses age directly as numeric input. Assumes linear effects. \\
  & Binned & Discretizes age into groups (e.g., decades) and maps each bin to a learned embedding. Captures nonlinear and threshold effects. \\
\midrule
Lab sequence encoding
  & Raw values & Uses observed biomarker values directly. Preserves absolute clinical scale and keeps preprocessing minimal. \\
  & Within-sequence normalization & Per-sequence z-scoring with learnable scale and shift, then denormalized at output. Stabilizes optimization and improves cross-analyte sharing. \\
\midrule
Training objective
  & Gaussian NLL & Predicts mean and log-variance under a normal distribution. Aligns with conventional RI assumptions. May underperform for skewed or heavy-tailed labs. \\
  & Quantile (pinball) loss & Predicts fixed quantiles (2.5, 25, 50, 75, 97.5\%). Distribution-free, robust to skewness and heteroscedasticity. \\
